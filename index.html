04 / Workflow

From prompt to
final frame.

A flexible process that combines fast AI exploration with deliberate, hands-on editorial decisions.

  1. 01

    Creative direction

    I shape the idea into a focused concept, story structure, mood, shot plan, and set of production goals.

  2. 02

    AI visual development

    I develop and refine prompts, images, and motion so the generated material stays visually coherent and supports the story.

  3. 03

    Editorial craft

    I build the sequence through shot selection, pacing, motion graphics, transitions, music, and detailed sound design.

  4. 04

    Polish & delivery

    I finish with color, cleanup, audio polish, quality control, and exports tailored to the intended platform.
