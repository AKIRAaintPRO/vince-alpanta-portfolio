01 / About Me

Story first.
Tools second.

Editing gives every generated image, raw clip, sound cue, and transition a reason to be there.

I create AI-assisted advertisements, UGC-style videos, social content, explainers, and cinematic short-form visuals.

My approach starts with the story, not the tool. I shape pacing, shot choice, sound, motion, and color by hand, then use AI where it can extend the visual possibilities or make exploration more flexible.

I am based in Digos City, Philippines and open to remote freelance, part-time, full-time, and ongoing creative opportunities.

06 / FAQ

Questions?
Good.

If your project is unusual, the best next step is still a direct conversation.

What types of videos do you edit?+

Short-form social content, talking-head videos, UGC ads, AI-generated content, promotional videos, podcast edits, motion-led pieces, and cinematic edits.

What files do you need?+

Usually the source footage, script or brief, brand assets, reference links, and delivery requirements. The exact checklist depends on the project.

How long does an edit take?+

Timing depends on runtime, footage volume, complexity, and revision needs. I will confirm a realistic schedule after reviewing the brief.

How many revisions are included?+

Revision scope is agreed before the project begins so the expectations stay clear for both sides.

Can you edit vertical and horizontal video?+

Yes. Deliverables can be prepared for vertical, square, or landscape platforms according to the agreed brief.

Do you create AI-generated content?+

Yes. I can develop AI-generated imagery and video sequences, then integrate them through hands-on editing, motion, sound, and color.

How are final files delivered?+

Files are exported to the formats agreed for each platform. The specific transfer method is confirmed per project.

Are you available for long-term projects?+

I am open to freelance, part-time, full-time, and ongoing remote opportunities. Send the project details to discuss fit and availability.
